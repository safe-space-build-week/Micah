Mental Health

Save your mental health with Safe Space, an app that reminds you that you are not alone, and that you are capable.

Depression

(noun) - feelings of severe despondency and dejection.

With Safe Space, you can help yourself keep depression at bay.

Anxiety

(noun) - a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease, typically about an imminent event or something with an uncertain outcome.

Safe Space will help you remember that you can overcome your anxiety.

How It Helps

With Safe Space, you can sign up for daily messages curated by yourself to give you encouragment throughout the day. These messages can be simple quotes, your favoirte memories, or a list of people that believe in you.

How To Start

Simply follow the instructions below to get signed up for Safe Space. You will enter in your information, and then edit the messages you want sent and when you want them received. Try to plan ahead and send messages when you think you'll need them the most!

Sign Up

Click on the sign up buttom below to get started. Simply add your information and follow the instrutions, and we will deliver you your personalized messages at the specified times.
